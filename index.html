About Us

StudiumOS is your ultimate productivity companion, designed to streamline the way students, professionals, and creators work and learn. This all-in-one suite brings together a powerful collection of tools—including countdown timers, to-do lists, sticky notes, a scientific calculator, unit converters, and even a fun game widget—to keep you organized and focused. With its sleek, customizable interface, StudiumOS adapts to your unique workflow. Arrange and resize widgets to suit your needs, switch between light and dark modes, and personalize your workspace with custom backgrounds and accent colors. Whether you're tackling assignments, managing projects, or sketching ideas, StudiumOS offers a seamless, distraction-free experience. StudiumOS is built with a passion for efficiency and is continuously evolving with new features and enhancements. Join our community to boost your productivity and help shape the future of this innovative platform!
